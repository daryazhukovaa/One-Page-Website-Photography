About Me

Hey, I'm Alex Morgan

I'm a professional photographer based in New York with over 10 years of experience capturing life's most precious moments. My journey in photography began when I was just 16, and since then, it has evolved from a passion into a career I deeply love.

My style combines creative composition with natural lighting to create timeless, emotive images. I believe every photo should tell a story and evoke genuine emotion.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me hiking in nature, exploring new coffee shops, or spending time with my family and our golden retriever, Max.
